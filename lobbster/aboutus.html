About Us

Welcome to our collaborative platform, a joint endeavor between Albany Law School and SUNY Albany, institutions esteemed for their academic rigor and technological advancements.

Under the guidance of Ray Brescia from Albany Law and Christopher Velez from SUNY Albany, this project has been realized through meticulous planning and execution.

The content of this website was curated by James Hoose and Lauren Wilt from Albany Law, whose expertise has contributed to the depth and quality of the information presented.

The website's design and functionality are the result of the skillful work of Jocelyn Chan, Jesse Conklin, Matthew Galletta, and Gyuhaeng 'Alex' Lee. Updates were made by James Corona, Watson Kim, Isadora Morandi Cabrera, and Oleg Balitskiy from SUNY Albany. Their technical proficiency has ensured a smooth and user-friendly experience.

We appreciate your visit to our platform and thank you for your interest in our work.
