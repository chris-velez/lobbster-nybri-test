The New York Bank Ratings Index is a quick and easy-to-use tool designed to help New Yorkers find a bank that best suits them. First developed in 2017, it uses 20 different categories for rating the largest banks operating in New York. After a user assigns weights to these categories indicating his or her personal preferences with respect to those categories, the user can generate a list of best banks for that individual.
